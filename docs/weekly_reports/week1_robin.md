## ✅ Achievements
### 1. Hardware & Infrastructure Audit
* **Main Lab Server (147.46.234.66):** Analyzed physical and electrical constraints. Ruled out this option due to a lack of physical space and suitable PCIe slots.
* **Alternative Workstation (Ryzen 9 9950X, TITAN X):** Conducted a comprehensive diagnostic, which highlighted several critical bottlenecks:
    * **OS:** Incompatible (Ubuntu 24.04 installed vs. 22.04 LTS strictly required).
    * **Memory:** Insufficient (32 GB DDR5 present vs. 64 GB minimum required for the host).
    * **PCIe & Power Supply:** Primary PCIe slot occupied, restricted cooling airflow, and undersized PSU (Micronics 700W) to safely handle the card's 300W TBP.
* Drafted a formal hardware assessment report for Manager Kim requesting a hardware upgrade (OS downgrade, RAM expansion, new PSU) or a dedicated machine.
